HOI AN TRAVEL TOUR GUIDE

Bamboo Basket Boat Riding in the Water Coconut Jungle (price : 150.000VND)

Picking up time :
Anytime (from 8am to 5pm)
A fun and interesting way to experience the rural life in Hoi An and Vietnamese cuisine.

Details:

Pickup

Pick you up at the Meeting Point & move to the Water Coconut Jungle.

Action

Spend 50’ on the bamboo basket boat , navigate the small canals of the mangrove forest and coconut palms with a local and join some interesting activities in the mangrove palm jungle: boat spinning and dancing; singing karaoke; try throw the fishing nets with the locals; enjoy the traditional folk song performance and fishing crabs along the river…

Action

Ended tour and back to the Meeting Point .
